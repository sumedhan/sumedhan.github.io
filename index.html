About Me

I am Sumedha, a full stack web developer. I code in JavaScript and Ruby. I am interested in building apps that simplify people's lives.

How I became a developer

After spending five years defining the HR agenda for unionized workforce in factories and warehouses, I knew I wanted a change. I craved solving analytical problems and learning something new. I had learnt C++ in my undergraduate degree and I wanted to explore other technologies. I began with some very beginner workshops and online courses and I knew I was hooked. A few months later, I quit my job and signed up for Lighthouse Labs' immersive web development program.
