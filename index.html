About Me

I am Sumedha, a full stack web developer. I code in JavaScript and Ruby. I am interested in building apps that simplify people's lives.

HR to Web Development

How I became a developer

After spending five years defining the HR agenda for unionized workforce in factories and warehouses, I knew I wanted a change. I craved solving analytical problems and learning something new. I had learnt C++ in my undergraduate Mechanical engineering classes and I wanted to explore other technologies. I began with some very beginner workshops and online courses and I knew I was hooked.

A few months later, I quit my job and signed up for Lighthouse Labs' immersive web development program.

My Bootcamp Experience

The Web-dev bootcamp was one of the most intense and exciting 10 weeks of my life. I learnt many different frameworks, libraries and skills during my program. But the most important skill of all, I learnt to be a life-long learner.
